## Research Projects

### TALO

A trajectory-aligned local optimization framework for long-term, spatially adaptive correction in online reconstruction systems.

### TT-Occ

A test-time 3D occupancy modeling framework that integrates Gaussian-based scene representation with strong visual foundation models.

### 3D World Modeling with Vision Foundation Models

A broader research direction aiming to unify geometry, semantics, and temporal reasoning for online 3D understanding.
