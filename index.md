## About

I am a **PhD candidate** in the **School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science** at **The University of Queensland**.

My research interests lie broadly in:

- 3D computer vision
- Vision foundation models
- 3D scene reconstruction
- Occupancy prediction
- Unified 3D world modeling

My recent work focuses on leveraging foundation models to improve geometric understanding and online 3D scene modeling, particularly for complex outdoor and multi-camera settings.

## Research

I am interested in building robust and scalable systems that connect:

- feed-forward 3D reconstruction,
- geometry-aware world modeling,
- semantic understanding, and
- downstream perception for embodied or autonomous systems.

Representative directions include long-term geometric alignment, test-time scene modeling, and structured 3D representation learning from visual observations.
